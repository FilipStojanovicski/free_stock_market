About the Free Stock Market

The Free Stock Market is a virtual stock brokerage that simulates the real stock market. You can practice trading and holding real stocks and cryptocurrencies with their true prices. Accurate prices are updated every hour from the stock exchange so you can see your investments grow in real time. You never have to risk any of your real money on the marketplace, everything is done with virtual currency. So go ahead and invest in your favorite companies and test your predictions to see just how much money you can make!

Creating an Account to Get Started
To start using the Free Stock market you first need to register an account. Click Get Started below or click Register to create an account. You do not need to provide email verification to register.
Buying and Selling Stocks
Once you are registered you can go to the market by clicking Market. Everyone starts with a balance of 100,000 USD in virtual currency when they register. A table of all of the stocks available to purchase is found on the left. To purchase a stock, click purchase and enter the quantity of the stock you want to purchase. All of your owned stocks can be found on the right table. To sell an owned stock click sell and enter the quantity of the stock you want to sell.
Investing and Making Money
The value of all of your owned stocks can rise and fall according to the value of the stock on the market. Stock prices are updated every hour according to the latest market price. Your total net worth is shown on the top of the Market Page beside Net Worth. Your net worth is the total value of all of your owned stocks and available currency.
Compare your Net Worth on the Leaderboard
You can compare your Net Worth with the Net Worth of other Users by clicking on the Leaderboard. Your ranking is displayed at the top above the ranking of the Top 20 Users with their Net Worth.
